The Project 64 etext of the unofficial Bards Tale II City Maps.
Converted to etext by The Blue Dragon and The Dark One, obtained from
either The Ranch (Harold Robbins), WireNuts (Jim Dillard), or
Enterprise (Tom Hoot) BBS's in Austin TX. The original document was
called "BardsII.Map.dox", supplied by Tom Hoot <thoot@neosoft.com>.

BA2MAP10.TXT, July 1996, etext #52.
