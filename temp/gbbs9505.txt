                                 ABOUT THIS LIST

  This list is published on or near the first of each month. Please remember
  when providing information from it that as much as 10% of it changes each
  month. IF THE YEAR AND MONTH GIVEN ABOVE ARE NOT THE CURRENT ONES, YOU ARE
  USING OUT-OF-DATE INFORMATION.

  GBBS* is intended as a finding aid for those interested in accessing a BBS
  with genealogy information. Every effort is made to provide a list that is
  accurate and up-to-date. However, because of inconsistencies and inaccuracies
  in source information and lack of current information regarding some BBSs,
  no warranty is made as to the accuracy of individual listings and the
  compiler is not responsible for any damages that may be claimed as arising
  from any error in the list. Timely corrections will be made of errors
  reported directly to the compiler.

  the genealogy-related FidoNet echo conferences (see list below). The list
  is compiled from ORIGIN lines in messages reaching Washington, DC, plus
  information gleaned elsewhere. BBSs are dropped from the list after 120
  days with no message activity. Lack of coding DOES NOT necessarily mean
  a particular conference is not carried on a particular BBS and many of 
  the listed BBSs may carry many additional genealogy-related echoes.

                            OTHER GENEALOGY SERVICES

Many systems participate in the following special services. Because of the
difficulty in obtaining current information, they are also noted in this list.
In general, however, you can expect those BBSs which carry several genealogy-

participate in any of these services should contact the appropriate individual
in the list below. Some of these services are:

genealogy shareware and freeware programs and some demo programs. Participating
BBSs can be expected to have the latest versions of this software.

TAFEL MATCHING SYSTEM (TMS)
Matches surnames in a prepared list (called a "Tiny Tafel" or "TT") with
similar lists posted by others and returns a report of the names and
addresses of those searching the same surnames. For information on TMS see a
file called TMSUSER.*. Specifications for creating a Tiny Tafel are in a file
called TTSPEC.*. Stand-alone TT generaters are available as TTGENnn.* or
