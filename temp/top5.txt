Due to the closing of many 800# BBS' due to lack of funds, Ray's Top 10 800#
BBS' had to be changed to Ray's Top 5 800# BBS'.  This is the current list for
July 1993.

BBS Name              BBS Number              Comments
--------              ----------              --------

1)  M-Link            1-800-254-6587          Once again, M-Link is at the top
                                              of the chart at #1!  Due to its
                                              great files base, great message 
                                              base, and of course its free ac-
                                              cess to Internet it will probably
                                              keep this position for quite a 
                                              while, until another great BBS
                                              comes out with more to offer.  
                                              M-Link is still currently running
                                              on 1 line but the wait is well 
                                              worth it.  In order to upgrade to
                                              more lines, M-Link needs your dona-
                                              tions, so if you would like to have
                                              easier access to it send them in.
                                              Ask the sysop about where to send 
                                              the donations once you are on.

2)  SBA               1-800-859-4636          This is another one of the greats.
                                              At number 2, this BBS offers the 
                                              best message base that anyone could
                                              ever ask for.  With many different
                                              message forums you are bound to find
                                              any information that you need.  And 
                                              the people on this board are great,
                                              they are always willing to help if
                                              anyone ever has any problems.  With
                                              plenty of nodes running, you should 
                                              have no problem logging on to this
                                              BBS.

3)  Gulfline          1-800-235-4662          This is just one of those BBS' that
                                              you've just got to love.  It has one
                                              of the finest files bases available
                                              and the Sysop is the kindest person
                                              you could ever chat with over the 
                                              lines.  And not to mention the great
                                              message base.  With this combination,
                                              this BBS is sure to be in the Top 5
                                              for months to come.  In order to gain
                                              full access to Gulfline you must wait
                                              a few days while your registration 
                                              gets processed.

4)  African Studies   1-800-446-0396          This is a great BBS when it comes
                                              to both message bases and files  
                                              bases.  Although mainly for the
                                              exchanging of information about 
                                              Africa, this bbs is open to the
                                              general public and has quite a few
                                              files available for download.

5)  Hyundai           1-800-955-5432          This is a great BBS in the files
                                              department.  It contains 22 different
                                              areas loaded with files, although
                                              most are for Hyundai computer users,
                                              there are many available share-ware
                                              and public domain files also.  At this
                                              time, the BBS' message base is down 
                                              and the sysop is not responding to
                                              users who E-mail him.  So this may be
                                              the last month for this BBS being on 
                                              the Top 5 List.


If you have any 800# BBS' that you think should be on this list send me a message
on any of these BBS', except Hyundai, stating the name and the phone number of the 
BBS.  I will check it out and if it is good enough, it will be in the Top 5 List 
the next month.  The number must be an 800#, available to all area codes in the 
U.S., in other words, not just from a certain state or area code, and the BBS must
be completely free to the user, no charges of any kind.  If there are enough BBS   
numbers given to me, the list may go back to Top 10 BBS' instead of Top 5.  

Thank you for using Ray's Top 5 800# BBS' and enjoy!
