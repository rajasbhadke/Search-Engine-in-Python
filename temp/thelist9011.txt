      to specify the exact baud rate in your listing. These modems are

   General info for new Sysops adding bbs's!:

      If you are a new Sysop or are just wishing to add your bbs to TheList
      for the first time, please be aware that we NO LONGER accept additions
      via the PDSLO BBS. We have been spending hundreds of dollars per month
      verifying new bbs systems that the expense of running TheList has
      become incredible to say the least. Effective 12/1/89, ALL new bbs
      systems that are to be added MUST be done so by submitting them via
      U.S. Mail.  Enclosed within this ZIP file is a file called ADDBBS.TXT
      which SysOps of new bbs's to be added, must fill out and return. We are
      now requesting that sysops make some small donation of $10 or more
      to help offset the costs of maintaining TheList. The donation is a one
      time request, good for the life of your bbs. SysOps of existing bbs's or
      those who are submitting them now can ALWAYS request updates to be made
      of TheList reflecting changes to their bbs's AT NO ADDITIONAL CHARGE !.

      At any time after submitting your bbs for addition you can call our
      bbs at 516-938-6722 OR mail a simple note with the requested changes
      to our address listed in the file.
