             *************
             * GAME PLAY *
             *************

     THE PLAYER MUST MATCH THE COLOR OF
THE PLATFORMS WITH THE BORDER, WHILE
AVOIDING TOUCHING "HOT SPRINGS" OR
"FIREBALLS."  WHEN NECCESSARY, THE
PLAYER CAN ENTER "SUPER COOL TIME" WHICH
TURNS THEM INTO "COLD SPRINGS" AND
"SNOWBALLS," WHICH THE PLAYER MAY
ABSORB.
