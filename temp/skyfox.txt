SKYFOX Help Screen (Electronic Arts)

Computer Commands

(ESC)       Help

(T)         Tactical Display
(I), (J), (K) or (M) Move Cursor
(A)         Autopilot
(S)         Score
(R)         Installation report


Plane Controls

(BUTTON 0)  Fire button
(BUTTON 1)  Thrust
Both        Autopilot

(SPACE)     Switch scanner
(G)         Guided missile
(H)         Heat seeker
(0-9)       Select speed
(A)         Autopilot
(C)         Computer
