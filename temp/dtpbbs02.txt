                         DESKTOP PUBLISHERS' BBS LIST

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

The following boards are fully desktop oriented, or are known to have desktop 
publishing sections with files and/or clip art for download. Some have a 
message base or conference(s) devoted to desktop publishing.

This listing includes updates received as of February 1, 1991 (hence the 02 in 
the filename). Updates are generally issued on a monthly basis, depending on my 
deadlines and other time constraints. If you note any errors or omissions, 
please leave me a message in one of the many echo conferences, preferably in 
the PubNet or LaserPub Conference (Fido), the Desktop Publishing echo (GT), or 
the DTP conference (SmartNet). Alternatively, info can be mailed to me at 10626 
Samaga Drive, Oakton, VA 22124 or voiced to 703/281-9601.

Please feel free to upload this list to other boards you may visit. It's nice 
and small and certainly won't hurt your upload credits on boards that count 
such things. Also, the better the distribution of this list, the better the 
information in future editions will be.

The Usual Disclaimer: By request, I am including descriptions of board 
offerings where available (i.e., conferences, file specialties, etc.). In doing 
this, however, I must emphasize that the correctness of this list lies with 
those who contribute information to it. I have neither the time (or the money) 
to personally verify the status of each of these boards. All I have is the 
desire to help those in the desktop community help each other.

                                                             Thanks!
                                                             Frank Atlee
