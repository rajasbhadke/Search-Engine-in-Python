TOFU SCRAMBLER

1 pound of tofu
veggies: garlic, onions, carrots, peppers
spices: cumin, tumeric, salt, pepper, cayenne, nutritional yeast

Prepare veggies-cut and seperate into bowls
Saute-onions and garlic until soft
Add carrots, then peppers. Crumble tofu over top, stir in.
Add spices
* the cumin and cayenne give it kick
* the tumeric and yeast give it color

contributed by Justin Gorman
