1] BOOT DISK
2] PRESS (A) FOR COLOR  (B) FOR B/W       [I HAVE YET TO SEE A DIF.]
3] PRESS (B) TO FLY  [ BAD DEMO ]
4] PRESS (B) THREE TIMES QUICKLY          [ RAISES ELEVATORS ]
5] PRESS (->) [RIGHT ARROW] 16            TIMES QUICKLY
6] SOON THE PLANE WILL TAKE OFF           BY ITSELF
7] HIT (F) .. THE PLANE WILL BANK
8] LEVEL OFF [PRESS (G)]
9] PRESS (ESC).. A MENU APPEAR            [ WE WILL DISCUSS LATER ]
10] PRESS (ESC) AGAIN
11] PRESS (H) FIVE TIMES AND WAIT         FOR THE PLANE TO CRASH
12] THE SIMULATOR WILL RESET ITSELF
