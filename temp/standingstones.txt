STANDING STONES - Help Page (Electronic Arts)


While Exploring

(D)     Drop something
(E)     End game and save
(R)     Rest
(T)     Throw a spell
(U)     Use potion/scroll

(I)     Forward        (*)
(J)     Turn left
(K)     Turn right
(M)     Turn around

* Use with CTRL to move through doors


While under attack

(B)     Bribe
(F)     Fight
(G)     Greet
(T)     Throw a spell
(J), (K) or (M) to attempt evasion


At any time

(ESC)   Help screen
(SPACE) Stats
