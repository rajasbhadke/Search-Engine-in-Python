NotebookThree.Unnumbered.Untitled-N

by j z provo

			6 oct 93

Enter a room
silent people,
  violent people,
and sit down. we've been
waiting.
 
you know us and yet ignore us,
we prance and dance for you;
your grey lives being what we are taught
to desire.
 
we resign --with this meeting--
and you'll have to learn
to entertain yourSelves.
